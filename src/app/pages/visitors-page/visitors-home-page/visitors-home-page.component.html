NEXT STEPS


Get connected to New Creation

If you would like to know more about what it means to be a member of this church family, please contact the church office, or join us at our Connect Table at our Connect Sunday celebration. Meet some of the elders and pastors, ask questions and enjoy a cup of coffee and a scone together with others. Connect Sundays will be advertised in our monthly newsletter and/or during notices. You can register ahead of time with the church office, or arrive at the table on the day.


Participate in our Heart & Soul seminar

After attending the Church for a period of time, we encourage those new to the Church to join our Heart and Soul seminar. This course will be hosted on two consecutive Sunday mornings, once a term, at the same time as a Sunday celebration. Heart and Soul gives basic foundational principles of what we believe as a Church in an interactive seminar. This seminar is optional, though we encourage everyone to participate.
