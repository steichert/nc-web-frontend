NC SonKidz believes that no one is too young to have a living relationship with Jesus Christ and aims to nurture and disciple children in the ways of God. We believe in having fun in a safe and loving environment. NC SonKidz hope to reach out to our community in a way that shows the practical love of Jesus to the children in our church. NC SonKidz achieves this through a simple parent led, together with responsible helpers, program that consists of: singing songs, bible story based lessons, fun dramas and puppet shows, and lesson-focused activities! NC SonKidz is the next step after Babies Bible Class, and is for children 3 - 4 years old. It is a simple and effective programme, structured around a chosen curriculum that will build key foundations and biblical principles into your child's life, through Bible stories, songs, etc.




NC SonKidz Model



REGISTRATION


NC Kidz begins with the registration process (around 08h45). Parents, upon entering the church, register their children and babies, for any of the NC Kidz ministries. First we ask that all parents register their children via our database form or online via our database link. This is for security, administration, and control purposes. Each child or baby then goes and receives a paper name bracelet (that cannot be removed by the child) in the colour of the ministry they are attending and/or NC Kidz Passport, depending on the ministry they are attending. Parents and children then go into the auditorium. We feel that it is important to worship the Lord as a family and for the babies and children to watch and learn from their parents to see how to praise the Lord. At various stages babies and children are released according to their various ages and ministries. Before entering their respective venues, Children are checked to ensure they all have a bracelet. At the end of the service time parents can fetch their children upon which the bracelets are cut off.




NC SONKIDZ


The NC SonKidz program begins once all the kids have arrived. ‘On duty’ parents and volunteers lead a program that consists of:

  • Praise and Worship - Praise and Worship gets the kids singing and worshipping in order to honor God and ushering in His presence!
  • Teaching -  A teaching is given based on the theme to get the kids thinking and equipping them with knowledge. Teachings consist of a story, skit, puppet show or interactive activity.
  • Craft Time -  The kids make some kind of craft, picture , object that reminds them of what they have learnt. This get stuck into an exercise book which the child may take home at the end of the term.
  • Snack Time - Juice and biscuits are given out.
  • Play Time - There is a fully equipped playground with slides, swings, jungle gyms and a sandpit where the kids may play until the end of the church service and are then collected by their parents.



ROSTER ROTATION


We ask each parent to serve on a roster and remain for the duration of the morning. In addition to the parent on duty, we are glad that some responsible teenagers are keen to serve in this ministry. They are trained as part of the team to ensure happy Sunday meetings together.

Duties include:

8:45   - Arrive, set-up, and prayer in the classroom.
9:00   - Worship with family in the auditorium.
9:40   - Return to the play-area and receive and settle families.
9:45   - Facilitate the program.
9:55   - Serve juice and biscuits and oversee playtime.
10:30 - Open gate, scan membership cards and ensure handover of the children to parents.
10:45 - Tidy up. Place everything back in the boxes and leave the classroom the way that you found it. We have a boxes in which we keep all equip for the use of the children.



In addition to the host and the parent on duty, we are glad that some responsible teenagers are keen to serve in this ministry. They are trained as part of the team to ensure happy Sunday meetings together.
