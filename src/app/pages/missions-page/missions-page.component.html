SYNERGY IN MISSION

ABOUT US


Synergy in Mission (SIM) represents the various ministries and initiatives that New Creation Family Church is serving in God’s mission both locally and around the world. Synergy describes the ongoing collaboration toward the completion of Christ’s Great Commission. The SIM Team meets regularly for prayer, planning and action. Sharon Stephen serves and the SIM Coordinator.

Please contact admin@newcreation.co.za to find out more about the SIM vision, ministries and how you can get more involved.
